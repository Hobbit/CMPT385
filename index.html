Welcome to Team Red Panda

Team Red Panda is a 4 member development team who's aim is to create apps for their CMPT 385 class. The focus of the apps is to create educative games that utilize word lists provided by the CANIL Linguistics Institute. These word lists are designed to promote the literacy at an accelerated rate.
